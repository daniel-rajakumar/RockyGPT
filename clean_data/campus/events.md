## ğŸ‰ **Major Annual Events**

### Fall Semester

#### Welcome Week (Late August)
- **What:** Week-long orientation for new students
- **Events:** Ice cream social, club fair, movie nights, mixer events
- **Who:** All students, especially freshmen
- **Organized by:** CSI & Student Life

#### Club Fair (September)
- **What:** Explore 100+ student clubs and organizations
- **Location:** Usually outdoor during good weather
- **Who:** All students
- **Why:** Join clubs, meet people, get involved

#### Homecoming (October)
- **What:** Weekend celebration with alumni
- **Events:** Football game, carnival, comedy show, alumni gatherings
- **Who:** Students, alumni, families

#### Halloween Events (Late October)
- **What:** Costume contests, haunted events, movie screenings
- **Organized by:** Various clubs and CSI

#### Stress Relief Week (December)
- **What:** Events before finals to help students relax
- **Events:** Therapy dogs, free snacks, yoga, game nights
- **When:** Week before finals

---

### Spring Semester

#### Spring Fest / Ramapo Fest (April)
- **What:** Major spring celebration
- **Events:** Live music, food trucks, carnival games, performances
- **Location:** Campus quad/outdoor areas
- **Who:** Entire campus community
- **One of the biggest events of the year!**

#### Earth Week (Late April)
- **What:** Environmental awareness events
- **Events:** Sustainability fair, tree planting, clean-ups
- **Organized by:** Environmental clubs

#### Cultural Events (Throughout semester)
- **What:** Celebrations of diverse cultures
- **Events:** Cultural food nights, performances, speakers
- **Examples:** Black History Month, Hispanic Heritage, Asian Pacific Islander Month

#### End of Year Events (May)
- **What:** Celebration before finals and graduation
- **Events:** Senior week, awards ceremonies
- **Graduation:** Late May commencement ceremony
