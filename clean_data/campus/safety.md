## ðŸƒ **What to Do in Different Emergencies**

### Medical Emergency
1. Call Public Safety: **201-684-6666**
2. Or call 911
3. Provide location and nature of emergency
4. Stay with person until help arrives

### Fire
1. **Pull fire alarm**
2. **Evacuate building immediately**
3. Call Public Safety: **201-684-6666**
4. Do NOT use elevators
5. Meet at designated assembly point
6. Do NOT re-enter until cleared

### Suspicious Person/Activity
1. Call Public Safety: **201-684-7432**
2. Or emergency: **201-684-6666**
3. Provide description and location
4. Do not approach or confront

### Active Threat (Lockdown)
1. **RUN** - If safe exit is available
2. **HIDE** - If you can't escape
   - Lock doors
   - Turn off lights
   - Silence phones
   - Stay out of sight
3. **FIGHT** - As last resort only
4. Call 911 when safe

### Weather Emergency
1. Follow campus alerts
2. Seek shelter indoors
3. Avoid windows
4. Follow instructions from authorities
