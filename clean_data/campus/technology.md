## üì∂ **WiFi Connection**

### Network Name (SSID)
**Ramapo-Secure**

### How to Connect

#### All Devices
1. Select "Ramapo-Secure" network
2. Enter your Ramapo credentials:
   - **Username:** Your Ramapo username
   - **Password:** Your Ramapo password

#### Advanced Settings (Chrome OS / Android)
- **EAP Method:** PEAP
- **Phase 2 Authentication:** MSCHAPv2
- **CA Certificate:** "Do not check" or "Use system certificates"

#### Apple Devices (iPhone / Mac)
- Accept the certificate when prompted

> **Tip:** If you have trouble connecting, contact the ITS Help Desk at 201-684-7777

### Common Issues

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
