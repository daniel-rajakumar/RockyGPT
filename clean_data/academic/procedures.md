### How to Register for Classes

1. **Check Your Registration Date**
   - Log into Web Self-Service (ssba.ramapo.edu)
   - View your assigned registration date and time
   - Cannot register before your assigned time

2. **Plan Your Schedule**
   - Review course offerings in Web Self-Service
   - Check prerequisites for courses
   - Consult with your academic advisor if needed

3. **Register**
   - Log into Web Self-Service on or after your registration date
   - Search for courses by subject, course number, or CRN
   - Add courses to your cart
   - Submit registration

4. **Confirm Registration**
   - Check your schedule in Web Self-Service
   - Verify all courses are listed
   - Note your class times and locations

> **Contact:** Registrar at 201-684-7695 or registrar@ramapo.edu

---

### How to Add/Drop a Class

#### Adding a Class
1. Log into Web Self-Service
2. Go to "Registration"
3. Select "Add/Drop Classes"
4. Search for the course
5. Click "Add" and submit

#### Dropping a Class
1. Log into Web Self-Service
2. Go to "Registration"
3. Select "Add/Drop Classes"
4. Select the course to drop
5. Click "Drop" and submit
6. Confirm the drop

**Important Deadlines:**
- **Add Deadline:** Typically end of first week (check academic calendar)
- **Drop Deadline (no W):** Typically end of second week
- **Withdrawal Deadline (with W):** Mid-semester (check academic calendar)

> **Tuition Refund:** Dropping before the 100% refund deadline = full refund. After that, partial or no refund.

---

### How to Withdraw from a Class

**After the drop deadline, withdrawals receive a "W" grade**

1. Consult with your academic advisor first
2. Consider impact on financial aid and full-time status
3. Complete withdrawal form (available from Registrar)
4. Submit to Registrar's Office
5. Receive confirmation

**Deadline:** Check academic calendar for withdrawal deadline (usually mid-semester)

> **Warning:** Withdrawing may affect financial aid, scholarships, and full-time status

## ðŸ“„ **Academic Records**

### How to Request Transcripts

#### Official Transcripts
1. Visit the [National Student Clearinghouse](https://www.studentclearinghouse.org/)
2. Select "Order Transcripts"
3. Search for "Ramapo College"
4. Create account or log in
5. Complete order form
6. Pay processing fee ($10-15)
7. Choose delivery method (email, mail, or pick-up)

#### Unofficial Transcripts
1. Log into Web Self-Service
2. Select "Academic Records"
3. Click "View Unofficial Transcript"
4. Print or save as PDF

**Processing Time:** 
- Electronic: 1-2 business days
- Mail: 5-7 business days

> **Contact:** Registrar at 201-684-7695

---

### How to Get a Degree Audit

1. Log into Web Self-Service
2. Select "Academic Records"
3. Click "Degree Audit" or "Degree Works"
4. View your progress toward degree
5. See completed requirements and what's remaining

**Use This To:**
- Track graduation requirements
- Plan future semesters
- Identify missing requirements
- Explore "what-if" scenarios for changing majors

## ðŸŽ“ **Major & Graduation**

### How to Change Your Major

1. **Research New Major**
   - Review requirements on Ramapo website
   - Consider prerequisites and time to complete

2. **Meet with Advisors**
   - Schedule meeting with your current advisor
   - Meet with advisor in the new major department

3. **Complete Change of Major Form**
   - Obtain form from Registrar or online
   - Get required signatures
   - Submit to Registrar's Office

4. **Confirmation**
   - Receive confirmation email
   - New major updated in system
   - Assigned new advisor

> **Contact:** Registrar at 201-684-7695 or your academic advisor

---

### How to Apply for Graduation

**Apply in your final year, typically during your last fall semester**

1. **Check Eligibility**
   - Review degree audit to confirm you'll meet all requirements
   - Ensure you'll complete 128+ credits
   - Verify GPA requirements

2. **Apply Online**
   - Log into Web Self-Service
   - Select "Apply for Graduation"
   - Choose your graduation term
   - Submit application

3. **Pay Graduation Fee**
   - $100 graduation application fee
   - Billed to your student account

4. **Attend Graduation Meetings**
   - Watch for emails about graduation information sessions
   - Learn about commencement ceremony details

5. **Confirmation**
   - Receive confirmation of graduation application
   - Degree audit reviewed by Registrar

**Application Deadlines:**
- **May Graduation:** Apply by October 15
- **December Graduation:** Apply by March 15

> **Contact:** Registrar at 201-684-7695 or registrar@ramapo.edu

## ðŸ’° **Financial & Account Management**

### How to View Your Bill

1. Log into Web Self-Service
2. Select "Student Account"
3. Click "Account Summary" or "View Bill"
4. Review charges and credits
5. Note payment deadline

### How to Make a Payment

**Online Payment:**
1. Log into Web Self-Service
2. Select "Student Account"
3. Click "Make a Payment"
4. Enter payment amount
5. Enter bank account or card information
6. Submit payment

**Other Payment Options:**
- Mail check to Bursar's Office
- In-person at Bursar (D-Wing, First Floor)
- Payment plan (enroll at start of semester)

> **Contact:** Bursar at 201-684-7495 or studentaccts@ramapo.edu

---

### How to Set Up a Payment Plan

1. Log into Web Self-Service during enrollment period
2. Select "Payment Plan Enrollment"
3. Review plan options and terms
4. Enroll in preferred plan
5. Pay enrollment fee
6. Set up autopay for monthly installments

**Typical Plans:**
- 4-month plan (Fall/Spring)
- 2-month plan (Summer)
- Small enrollment fee ($30-50)
