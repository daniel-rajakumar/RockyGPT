## ðŸ’³ **Payment Options**

### 1. **Pay in Full**
- Online via Web Self-Service
- By mail (check to Bursar's Office)
- In person at Bursar (D-Wing, First Floor)

### 2. **Payment Plan**
- **4-month plan** (Fall/Spring semesters)
- **2-month plan** (Summer)

- **Enroll:** Through Web Self-Service at start of semester

### 3. **Financial Aid**
- Grants, loans, and scholarships applied automatically
- Reduces amount you owe
- Check aid status in Web Self-Service

## ðŸ’µ **Refund Policy**

### 100% Refund Period
- Typically **first week** of classes
- Drop classes by deadline for full tuition refund

### Partial Refund
- **Week 2-3:** 50-75% refund (varies)
- **After week 3:** Usually no refund

### Non-Refundable Fees
- Parking permits (after 100% refund period)
- Course fees (after 100% refund period)
- Graduation application fee
- Transcript fees
- All other administrative fees

> **Always check the academic calendar for exact refund deadlines!**
